-MY STYLE-

We specialize in weddings + portrait photography and all types of photography ranging from corporate photos, indoor photography , outdoor photography.

wildlife photography I create photos that are emotional, bold, rich, moody, romantic, whimsical, and fun.

My goal is for my clients to have an enjoyable experience. I take a laid-back, fun approach to capturing the moments. Rather than forced smiles, I want to capture uncontrollable laughter and joy. I use an “unposed” method to achieve stunning photographs that are fluid, natural, and flattering.

My ideal clients are those who march to the beat of their own drum, who love to laugh and have fun, and appreciate art and having something a little different.

I love working with nature and finding the beauty that’s all around me. My favorite moments are captured by being creative and doing anything out of the ordinary.
Life is art. Let’s make it happen.
